## How Projects Work

Our project process is designed to be clear, collaborative, and stress-free from start to finish.

**Discovery and Planning** begins when you tell us what you need. We'll discuss your goals, requirements, and timeline, then provide you with a detailed quote that's valid for 30 days. Our quotes include everything needed to complete your project with no hidden costs or surprise charges.

**Getting Started** requires a deposit to secure your project slot and begin development. For smaller projects under £1,000, we require 50% upfront with the balance due on completion. Larger projects follow the same 50/50 structure, while substantial projects over £5,000 are split into three manageable payments to improve cash flow for both parties.

**Development and Collaboration** is where the magic happens. You provide the content, images, and feedback while we handle all the technical development. We'll keep you updated throughout the process and incorporate your feedback to ensure the final result exceeds your expectations.

**Launch and Handover** occurs once your website is complete and final payment is received. At this point, your website is fully yours, and we'll provide you with all necessary login credentials and documentation.
