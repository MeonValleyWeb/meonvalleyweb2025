## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

When you contact us or use our services, we collect information you provide directly, including your contact information (name, email address, phone number, company name), project information such as details about your website requirements and specifications, and communication records from our emails and messages. For hosting services, we collect account information including login credentials and preferences. Payment information such as billing addresses is processed securely through third-party payment processors.

### Information Collected Automatically

Our website automatically collects certain information about your visit, including website usage data such as pages visited, time spent, browser type, and device information. We also collect technical data including your IP address, browser settings, and operating system. Some of this information is collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies, as detailed in our Cookie Policy below.

### Information from Third Parties

We receive limited information from third parties necessary for our services, including transaction confirmations and payment status from payment processors, server logs and performance data from hosting providers for websites we host, and website performance statistics from analytics services to help us improve our services.

## How We Use Your Information

### Legal Bases for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your personal data based on several legal grounds. We process data for contract performance when providing website design and hosting services you've requested. We rely on legitimate interests to improve our services and communicate about relevant offerings that may benefit you. For marketing communications and non-essential cookies, we obtain your consent. We also process data to meet legal obligations such as tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements.

### Specific Uses

We use your information primarily for service delivery, including creating, hosting, and maintaining your website. Your contact information enables us to provide customer support, respond to inquiries, and offer technical assistance. We process payment information for billing and maintain financial records as required by law. We analyze usage patterns to improve our services and may send relevant updates about our services with your consent. All processing is conducted in accordance with data protection laws and our commitment to your privacy.

## Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

### Access and Portability
- **Right to Access**: Request copies of your personal data
- **Data Portability**: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

### Correction and Deletion
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)

### Processing Controls
- **Right to Restrict**: Limit how we process your data
- **Right to Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent for marketing or cookies at any time

### Exercising Your Rights
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@meonvalleyweb.com. We will respond within one month.
