# Legal Documents

This directory contains the legal documents for Meon Valley Web in markdown format for easy editing and maintenance.

## Files

### privacy-policy.md
- **Purpose**: GDPR and UK data protection compliant privacy policy
- **Covers**: Data collection, processing, storage, user rights, cookies, international transfers
- **Compliance**: UK GDPR, Data Protection Act 2018, ICO guidelines
- **Last Updated**: January 2025

### terms-of-service.md
- **Purpose**: Comprehensive terms covering all services
- **Covers**: Website design/development, hosting, maintenance, payments, intellectual property
- **Services**: WordPress, Headless WordPress, AstroJS, Laravel, hosting plans
- **Last Updated**: January 2025

## Editing Guidelines

1. **Update Dates**: Always update the "Last updated" date when making changes
2. **Legal Review**: Consider legal review for significant changes
3. **Consistency**: Maintain consistent terminology across documents
4. **Compliance**: Ensure changes maintain GDPR/UK law compliance

## Deployment

These markdown files are automatically rendered as web pages:
- `/privacy` - Renders privacy-policy.md
- `/terms` - Renders terms-of-service.md

## Key Areas to Review Regularly

### Privacy Policy
- Data processing activities
- Third-party services and integrations
- Cookie usage and tracking
- Data retention periods
- Contact information

### Terms of Service
- Service descriptions and pricing
- Payment terms and schedules
- Liability limitations
- Dispute resolution procedures
- Contact information

## Compliance Notes

- **ICO Registration**: Ensure ICO registration is current if processing significant personal data
- **Cookie Consent**: Implement appropriate cookie consent mechanisms
- **Data Breach Procedures**: Maintain incident response procedures
- **Regular Reviews**: Review annually or when services change significantly
