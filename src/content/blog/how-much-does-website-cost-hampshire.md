If you're a Hampshire business owner researching website costs, you've probably seen prices ranging from £500 to £50,000. That's not particularly helpful when you're trying to budget.

As a Hampshire-based web design agency with 15+ years of experience, we've built hundreds of websites for local businesses. In this guide, we'll break down exactly what you can expect to pay—and what you actually get at different price points.

> **Quick Answer:** Most Hampshire small businesses pay between £1,500 and £5,000 for a professional brochure website. E-commerce sites typically range from £3,000 to £10,000+ depending on complexity.

## What Factors Affect Website Cost?

### Number of Pages
A simple 5-page site (Home, About, Services, Contact) costs less than a 20+ page site with complex navigation.

### E-commerce Functionality
Online stores require product pages, payment processing, stock management—adding significant complexity.

### Custom Design vs Template
Bespoke designs tailored to your brand cost more than modified templates—but convert better.

### Content Management System
WordPress sites cost differently than custom-built headless sites or page builder platforms.

## Website Pricing for Hampshire Businesses

| Website Type | Typical Cost | What You Get | Best For |
|--------------|--------------|--------------|----------|
| **DIY / Template** | £10-50/mo | Wix/Squarespace template, basic pages, limited customization | Micro businesses with no budget |
| **Brochure Website (5-10 pages)** | £1,500-3,500 | Custom design, mobile-responsive, CMS, basic SEO, contact forms | Most small businesses, trades, professionals |
| **Brochure Website (10-20 pages)** | £3,500-7,000 | Advanced design, portfolio/gallery, blog, enhanced SEO, animations | Growing businesses, tourism, professional services |
| **E-commerce (Small shop)** | £3,000-6,000 | WooCommerce setup, 20-50 products, payment gateway, basic shipping | Local retailers, craft businesses |
| **E-commerce (Medium shop)** | £6,000-15,000 | Advanced WooCommerce, 100+ products, custom features, integrations | Established retailers, multi-category stores |
| **Bespoke / Complex** | £7,000-20,000+ | Custom functionality, headless CMS, integrations, membership systems | Large businesses, unique requirements |

*Prices are estimates based on Hampshire market rates. Actual costs depend on specific requirements.*
