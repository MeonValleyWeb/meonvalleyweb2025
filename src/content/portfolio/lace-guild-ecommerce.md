# The Lace Guild Ecommerce Modernization

A comprehensive modernization project for The Lace Guild, transforming their established ecommerce platform and museum website to serve their community better in the digital age.

## Project Overview

The Lace Guild is a well-established organization dedicated to preserving and promoting the art of lace-making. This project involves modernizing their existing ecommerce platform while maintaining their rich heritage and community focus.

## Key Challenges

- **Legacy System Migration**: Carefully transitioning from outdated systems
- **Heritage Preservation**: Maintaining the organization's traditional values in a modern interface
- **Community Integration**: Serving both artisans and collectors effectively
- **Museum Functionality**: Combining ecommerce with educational content

## Planned Features

- **Modern Ecommerce Platform**: Streamlined shopping experience for lace-making supplies
- **Digital Museum**: Interactive galleries showcasing lace-making history and techniques
- **Community Hub**: Forums and resources for lace-making enthusiasts
- **Educational Resources**: Tutorials, patterns, and learning materials
- **Event Management**: Workshop and exhibition booking system
- **Member Portal**: Exclusive content and community features

## Technical Approach

- **Progressive Enhancement**: Gradual modernization to minimize disruption
- **Performance Focus**: Fast loading times for image-heavy content
- **Accessibility**: Ensuring the site serves users of all abilities
- **SEO Optimization**: Improving discoverability for the lace-making community
- **Mobile Optimization**: Responsive design for modern device usage

## Impact Goals

- Increase online engagement with the lace-making community
- Improve ecommerce conversion rates and user experience
- Preserve and digitize historical lace-making knowledge
- Expand the organization's reach to new generations of artisans
